Location : Chennai

Roles & Responsibilities :

The following processes were developed to fulfill this requirement:

• Charge Amortization Process: A batch process was developed to amortize the amortization amount from the suspense account to the P&L account.

• EIR/MIR Enquiry Process: A user-friendly process for enquiring the newly generated schedule, EIR, and MIR has been developed.
